What Cadence tracks · two passive channels at home

The camera watches movement, the mic listens to speech — the two symptom domains a doctor titrates against.

📹 Camera — movement
  • Tremor — rest-tremor frequency & amplitude
  • Gait — walking speed, arm swing, freezing
  • Rigidity / slowness — stiff, reduced movement

Why it matters: these rise and fall with every dose — they are the ON/OFF state a doctor is tuning.

🎙 Microphone — speech
  • Monotone — reduced pitch variation
  • Hypophonia — reduced loudness
  • Adherence — spoken medication check-ins

Why it matters: voice change is one of the earliest, most sensitive signs — often before movement declines.

both channels → one ON/OFF diary + titration flag
